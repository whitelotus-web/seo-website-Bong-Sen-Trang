# Viec tiep theo tren Google Business

## 1. Them dich vu

Vao `Chinh sua ho so` -> `Dich vu`, them cac dich vu sau:

```text
Lam bien quang cao tai Ha Noi
Thi cong bien quang cao
Bien alu chu noi
Bien hop den LED
Bang hieu cua hang
Bien quang cao nha hang quan an
Chu noi mica inox
Bien bat Hiflex
Pano bien tam lon
Bien vay cua hang
Sua chua bien quang cao
```

Mo ta tung dich vu nam trong file:

```text
GOOGLE-BUSINESS-ACTION-KIT.md
```

## 2. Upload anh

Vao `Anh` hoac `Them anh`, chon folder:

```text
F:\Dự Án\SEO Website Bong Sen Trang\google-business-upload
```

Thu tu nen upload:

```text
01-logo-bong-sen-trang.png
02-mau-bien-nha-hang-chu-noi.jpg
04-bien-chu-noi-mat-tien.jpg
05-bien-phat-sang-ngoai-troi.jpg
06-bien-hop-den-mat-tien.jpg
08-anh-thi-cong-thuc-te.jpg
```

## 3. Dang bai dau tien

Vao `Bai dang` hoac `Them thong tin cap nhat`, dung noi dung trong file:

```text
GOOGLE-BUSINESS-ACTION-KIT.md
```

Tieu de:

```text
Nhan lam bien quang cao tai Ha Noi
```

Anh nen chon:

```text
02-mau-bien-nha-hang-chu-noi.jpg
```

## 4. Them cau hoi dap hop le

Neu Google cho them Q&A, chi dung cau hoi dap that ve dich vu. Khong gia lam khach hang.

Lay cau hoi dap trong file:

```text
GOOGLE-BUSINESS-ACTION-KIT.md
```

## 5. Review

Khong tu review gia, khong tao review ao. Cach do co rui ro bi Google xoa review, ha uy tin ho so hoac khoa/tam dung ho so.

Thay vao do:

- Xin review tu khach that sau khi lam viec moi.
- Xin review tu khach cu neu con lien lac duoc.
- Neu khach ngai viet dai, gui mau goi y ngan trong `GOOGLE-BUSINESS-ACTION-KIT.md`.
- Moi cong trinh moi xong thi chup 3-5 anh va gui link xin review ngay.
